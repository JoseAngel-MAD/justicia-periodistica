Journalistic justice.

About this web.

This website is a tool to solve an unfair situation that happens in the press conferences of the Spanish Government. Only a few journalists have the option to ask question and not all of them, as they should.

The use of this website would allow all journalists to have the opportunity to speak and ask questions.

How many journalists are there?

How?

On a first-come, first-served basis, entry or any other method, journalists are given a number. The journalist will ask their question when their number appears on the screen.

The round of questions is over!.
